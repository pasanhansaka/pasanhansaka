## 🧠 About Me

> *"Clean code, creative design, and scalable architecture — that's the intersection I live in."*

I'm **Pasan Hansaka**, a passionate **Full-Stack Developer** based in 🇱🇰 Sri Lanka, focused on architecting robust enterprise systems and delivering polished user experiences. I bridge the gap between elegant backend logic and beautiful frontend design.

- 🏗️ Building enterprise-grade apps with **Jakarta EE**, **EJB**, and **Spring Boot**
- 📱 Engineering seamless cross-platform mobile experiences with **React Native** & **Android (Java)**
- 🎨 Designing **user-centric interfaces** that make complex systems feel simple
- 🌱 Constantly exploring new frameworks, design systems, and cloud architectures
- 🤝 Open to collaborations on **full-stack**, **mobile**, or **open-source** projects
